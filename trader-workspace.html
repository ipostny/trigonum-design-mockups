Momentum Breakout Strategy

I focus on high-timeframe breakouts (4H/Daily) on major pairs with strong volume confirmation.

Entry Rules

  • Wait for a clear breakout above/below key support/resistance on 4H chart
  • Volume must be >150% of 20-period average
  • RSI between 40-65 for longs, 35-60 for shorts
  • Funding rate must be neutral or favorable
  • Enter on first pullback to the breakout level

Exit Rules

  • TP at next major S/R level or 2-3x risk distance
  • SL below the breakout candle (longs) or above it (shorts)
  • Trail SL to breakeven after 1R move
  • Manual close if momentum fades near target zone

Risk Management

  • Max 1% risk per trade
  • Max 3 concurrent positions
  • Max 3x leverage on majors, 2x on alts
