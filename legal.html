Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the service provider ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our professional trading platform and related services (the "Platform"). This Platform is designed exclusively for professional trading teams, licensed entities, and authorized business users.

By accessing or using the Platform, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you register for the Platform, we collect information necessary to create and manage your account, including:

  • Name and business contact details
  • Email address
  • Organization name and role
  • Authentication credentials

2.2 Trading and Platform Data

In the course of providing our services, we collect and process:

  • Trading activity and transaction records
  • Platform configuration and preferences
  • API credentials for connected exchange accounts
  • Audit logs and system access records

2.3 Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information when you access the Platform:

  • IP address and device identifiers
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Access timestamps and session duration
  • Platform usage patterns and feature interactions

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining the Platform and its features
  • Processing and executing trading operations as instructed
  • Authenticating users and securing account access
  • Generating reports and analytics for authorized users
  • Communicating service updates and technical notifications
  • Maintaining audit trails and ensuring regulatory compliance

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • Regular security assessments and monitoring
  • Secure storage of API credentials using industry-standard encryption
